Symmetric and Unsymmetric Elements

Symmetric Elements

Symmetric elements are those that have balanced proportions and are identical on both sides when divided along a central axis. Examples of symmetric elements in design include:

  • Circle
  • Square
  • Equilateral Triangle
  • Butterfly Shape

Unsymmetric Elements

Unsymmetric elements lack balance and do not mirror each other on either side of a central axis. Examples of unsymmetric elements in design include:

  • Asymmetrical Shapes
  • Irregular Polygons
  • Abstract Forms
  • Natural Objects like Trees or Rocks

Understanding the distinction between symmetric and unsymmetric elements is essential for creating visually appealing and effective designs.
